# Malayalam Language Learning App - MVP Ideas

A collection of simple, beginner-friendly MVP ideas for a Malayalam language learning application.

---

## 1. Flashcard Word Trainer (Simplest)
- Show Malayalam words with English translations
- Swipe right/left or tap to mark as "known" or "learning"
- Track progress with a simple counter
- Categories: greetings, food, family, numbers, colors

## 2. Script Trainer (Alphabet Learning)
- Display Malayalam letters (vowels/consonants) one at a time
- Show stroke order animation or image
- Play pronunciation audio
- Quiz mode: "Which letter is this?"

## 3. Daily Word Widget
- One new word per day with pronunciation
- Push notification with the word
- Simple spaced repetition to review past words

## 4. Phrase Builder
- Common phrases for everyday situations (restaurant, travel, greetings)
- Tap to hear pronunciation
- Fill-in-the-blank exercises

## 5. Picture-Word Matching
- Show an image, pick the correct Malayalam word
- Great for visual learners and vocabulary building

## 6. Number Practice
- Learn Malayalam numbers 1-100
- Tap a number, see/hear Malayalam equivalent
- Quiz: "What is 47 in Malayalam?"

## 7. Conversation Simulator
- Pre-scripted dialogues (at a shop, meeting someone)
- Tap through conversation bubbles with translations
- Learn contextual phrases naturally

## 8. Word of the Day Journal
- Daily word with example sentence
- Users write their own sentence using the word
- Build a personal vocabulary journal

## 9. Malayalam Typing Tutor
- On-screen Malayalam keyboard
- Type what you see/hear
- Builds familiarity with the script

## 10. Audio Pronunciation Matcher
- Play a Malayalam word audio
- Pick the correct word from 4 options
- Trains listening comprehension

## 11. Verb Conjugation Trainer
- Show a verb, practice different tenses
- Fill in the correct conjugation
- Essential for sentence building

## 12. Mini Stories
- Short 5-10 sentence stories in Malayalam
- Tap any word for translation
- Gradual difficulty progression

## 13. Cultural Context Cards
- Malayalam phrases tied to festivals, customs, food
- Learn language through culture
- Shareable cards for social media

## 14. Speed Quiz Game
- Timed word recognition challenges
- Leaderboard/high score
- Gamified learning with streaks

## 15. Parent-Child Mode
- Simple words with colorful images for kids
- Parent speaks, child picks the image
- Family learning together

---

## Recommendation

**Start with the Flashcard Word Trainer** because:
- Minimal UI needed (card flip, category list)
- Immediate value for beginners
- Easy to expand later (add audio, spaced repetition, more categories)
